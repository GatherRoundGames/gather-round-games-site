Deep Dive into Rift Protocol

Tactical Squad Building

Assemble your team of elite soldiers, each with unique abilities, weapons, and protocols. Success hinges on synergistic team composition and precise tactical deployment.

  • Mission Focus: Defeat the horde and extract before the rift collapses.
  • Key features: Co-operative (mostly) tactical battle ground game. Using abilities, monitor cooldowns and eliminate as many of the horde as possible.

Survive the Horde

The rift is a dynamics, room tile placement battlefield. Combat decisions, escalating enemy numbers and traps will require both selfish and team actions to keep on top of.

  • Tension: Time ticks down until the Rift collapses and you left behind with the Horde.
  • Challenge: 3 tiers of enemies to claim as trophies of battle and team objectives to complete too. Best team or simply best hunter?
