Deep Dive into Spider Splat

Fast-Paced Fun for Everyone

In Spider Splat, players take turns flipping cards, searching for spiders, and racing to "splat" them with a special cup. Be quick and accurate to win spider tokens!

  • Player Count: Designed for 2-4 players (ages 4+).
  • Game Length: Quick 10-15 minute playtime.

Simple & Engaging Components

The game includes durable spider cards, special "splat" cups, and colorful tokens. All designed for small hands and quick, exciting gameplay.

  • Tactile Fun: Physical "splat" action for kids.
  • High Replayability: Every game is a new challenge.
