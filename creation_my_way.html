Impose Your Vision

A Duel of Gods!

Creation - My Way! isn't just a game; it's a tactical abstract dual. Two rival gods shape the same mortal realm. Each seeks to impose their vision upon the world — but only one vision will prevail.

Player Count 2 players
Ages 8 to 108
Type Tactical Board Control Dual

Strategy & Dominion

Make the world match your Creation card. Plan carefully, watch your opponents moves, destroy and create to make the world your way!

  • ✦ The Landscape: Wilds, Settlements and Shrines will make up your dominion.
  • ✦ Divine Race: Can you get them how you want before your opponent does?
