A cooperative card game set in the wild west. Work together as a posse to survive the treacherous western frontier and restore peace to the territory.

Gameplay & Features

A High-Stakes Co-op Adventure

In Trail's Edge, you'll join a posse to face the treacherous western frontier. Whether you're lawmen or outlaws, trouble is afoot, and you'll need to work together to overcome challenges, make tough choices, and survive. This co-op game is filled with narrative and strategic decisions, perfect for a team of players who want to test their wits and courage.

A Prototype in the Making

Trail's Edge is currently in the prototype phase, and we're looking forward to sharing more details soon. We're actively working on the game mechanics and narrative elements to ensure it delivers a challenging and engaging experience. Stay tuned for updates on our progress as we bring this western adventure to life!
