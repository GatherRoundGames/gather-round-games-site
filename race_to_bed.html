Race to Bed

A bedtime race which teaches children about bedtime routines in a fun, engaging and friendly way. Choose the order of your bed time tasks, race to get them done and then race to be the first in bed to win!

The Bedtime Race Adventure

Discuss Bedtime Routine Together

Talk about what we need to do to be ready for bed (and why if you like). Decide the order you want to complete the tasks independently. Race from room to room to complete your bedtime routine before the other players. Once you are ready time to get to bed!

  • Player Count: 2-4 players (Ages 4+).
  • Game length: 15-20 minutes.

Colourful Components

The print and play game features a modular game board, child-safe paper player tokens, and large, easy-to-read cards. The components are designed for self-standing and easy to assemble to ensure a fun, tactile experience for younger players.

  • Easy Setup: Get playing in under a minute.
  • Family Friendly: Perfect for introducing young children to cooperative strategy.
