Deep Dive into Race to Bed

A Fun and Cooperative Game for Kids

In Race to Bed, players plan their steps, race to complete their bedtime tasks, and get into bed before the others. The game encourages kids to talk about their routines and makes a typically difficult process enjoyable. It's a game that helps create positive habits through fun and play.

A Playtesting Journey

We're currently in the playtesting phase for Race to Bed, gathering feedback from families to ensure the game is as fun and effective as possible. The goal is to make a game that not only entertains but also genuinely helps with the bedtime struggle. We're excited to see how it helps families everywhere!

  • Player Count: Designed for 1-4 players (ages 2+).
  • Game Length: Quick 10-15 minute playtime.
